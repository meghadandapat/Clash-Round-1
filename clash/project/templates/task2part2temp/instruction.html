GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS
  • The game is 28 minutes long.
  • There is negative marking and the marking scheme for any question will be shown on screen.
  • Refreshing the page at any point of time is strictly prohibited
  • Cheating of any sort is strictly prohibited. Switching tabs and windows will lead to direct disqualification.
  • There are 3 lifelines whose rules are mentioned on this page.
  • You can check out these instructions at any point of time during the game as well.

LIFELINE #1

Get It Assured
  • A progress bar will be visible on top of the screen which will increase or decrease by a certain amount depending on the correctness of your answer.
  • Once the progress bar is full, you can use the Get Assured button on screen at any time to get 2 chances to answer that particular question.
  • The marking scheme changes for that question for which you have opted this lifeline which will be visible on the screen.
LIFELINE #2

Spin The Wheel
  • There will be a checkpoint number visible on top of your screen.
  • If your score is a multiple of the checkpoint score, the spin wheel button will get activated.
  • Once you spin the wheel, you will get some advantage or disadvantage depending on where the wheel stops.
  • This lifeline can be used only 2 times at the most.
LIFELINE #3

Visionise Yourself
  • Initially as you press the proceed button here, you will be prompted to enter your estimate predicted score.
  • You can enter the score or press cancel and enter your estimate later by pressing on the predicted score animation on screen.
  • Note that you can only enter the score during the first 5 minutes of the game after which the lifeline will be permanently disabled.
  • If your score is in +5 or -5 range of your score, you will see a button with which you can avail this lifeline.
  • You will able to play 3 extra questions which can give you bonus points. Opting this lifeline is completely optional but once you avail it, you will have to attempt at most 2 questions for sure.
